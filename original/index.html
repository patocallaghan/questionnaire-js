Welcome to our Export-Ready Questionnaire

We’ve put together a few simple questions to help you (and us) understand where you are on your export journey, and how we can help you reach your goals.

Along the way, we’ll point you in the direction of any relevant articles and resources on our website.

If you wish to be contacted by someone from our customer team at the end of this survey, an email summary of the information you’ve provided will be sent to our team. Please be assured that any information you provide here will be kept confidential, and used only by NZTE for contacting you and the possible provision of services to you and your organisation.
